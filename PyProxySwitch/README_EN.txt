PyProxySwitch  Version: 3.5.2

Description: PyProxySwitch is a cross-platform proxy switcher based on 3proxy and IP Relay
to fast change proxy for browsers(Firefox,Chrome,Opera,IE,etc.) and other Internet applications,
writen in Python and PyQt. Welcom to send me your feedback if you feel it useful.
